No Tanks Left
  • Local Multiplayer
  • Party Game
No Tanks Left is a game I completed for a school project. The game is played by only using one button, which turns you right. The tanks will constantly move and shoot. The game can fit as many players as there are keys on a keyboard. I was responsible for coding and designing the game, and my partner helped create some of the art assets.
The Patient
  • 3D Immersive Horror
  • Puzzle
The Patient was a game I worked on with a close friend of mine. The goal of the game is to make your way through the depths of an abandon hospital and escape. However, you are not alone. I was reponsible for coding and my partner created the story and the map.

Hotline Elf
  • Action Shooter
  • Game Jam
Hotline Elf is a game I completed in one day at the Tilted Shed Studios Game Jam Event. The two theme words were "eating" and "elf". Our game revolves around eating other elves, using special moves, and gaining power ups. We were even able to add a boss. We managed to tie for first place.
Space Space Manager
  • Management / Simulation
  • Game Jam
Space Space Manager is a game I completed in two days with a friend of mine. The theme of the jam was "Space". The gameplay revolves around managing the inventory of your ship by making sure you have enough fuel to make it to the next planet, but also keeping the most value items possible to make money. We got 5th place.
